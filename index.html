About Me

Welcome, stranger. Here's a "fun" summary about me:

  • 4th year CPSC student at UBC, proficient in C++ and Lua, currently working as a Junior Software Engineer at Skybox Labs on Stela!
  • Released a dimension estimation game (Paper Cut) on Play Store.
  • Lead a team of 6 people to create a tower defense/platformer game and it's engine from scratch using C++ and OpenGL, architected using the ECS pattern.
  • Strong foundation in algorithms and data structures:
    • Relevant courses include Intermediate Algorithm Design and Analysis and Problem Solving in Computer Science.
    • Relevant studying includes over 100 LeetCode problems solved and documented, basic data structures from heaps to AVL trees and basic sorting algorithms like mergeSort and quickSort implemented from scratch in C++
  • Volunteered at SIGGRAPH 2019!
  • I love playing all kinds of games, but here are some of my favorites: LoL, Borderlands, TF2, SSBM, SSBU, Soul Calibur 2, Murloc RPG, Sonny 2, Shining Soul II, Dream Of Mirror Online, Slay the Spire, but my absolute favorite game is WC3, particularly because of the custom maps.
  • Modest gaming achievements include:
    • Peak rank in LoL was Diamond 5, Season 4.
    • Won an amateur SSBU tournament of 40 people.

And here's my resume.

While I love games of all kinds, my focus is in those that encourage player interaction, creativity and experimentation. I believe the spirit of play and fun is in the choices that players get to make and the experiences that they get to create themselves. One of my favorite examples of this is games like Paper Mario. The simple act of challenging the player to time a button press makes even typical bland RPG combat against mobs fun!

Hence why I love games like WC3 that offer tools that make it easy for people to make games, since I believe making a game is the ultimate expression of player choice. That's why I really love to discover new kinds of games made by other people! Some great custom maps and examples: Warlocks, Enfos, Smashcraft, Pyramid Escape, Uther Party, Dark Deeds, Island Defense, Island Troll Tribes.

If you have any questions or inquiries, email trevinwong@gmail.com.
